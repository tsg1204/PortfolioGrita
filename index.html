About

Tatiana Grigorieva

Currently a software developer with Cotton, Inc., I was born and raised in Russia where I graduated from Smolensk Economic College with a degree in Accounting. My career began with the Center of Statistics as a computer operator where my interest and knowledge of computer programming began.

After moving to United States I worked as analyst for West Corp. where I learned the skills of application programming. Later over the years of different employments and volunteer work I was introduced to web development.

In my current position since March 2015. I'm working on maintenance and updates for a suite of software programs and services that leverage USDA High Volume Instrument (HVI) data, on building new features to add to the system which provides significantly improved cotton inventory management and analysis capabilities, as well as electronic communication between producers, ginners, mills, and merchants/co-ops.
